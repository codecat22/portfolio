Academic Papers & Articles

    January 2021

    Big Mood

    This paper focuses on the role of design tools used in professional settings, moodboards in particular, in online spaces created and curated by non-designers around a particular atmosphere and a desired emotional disposition or appeal.

    September 2021

    Standing On Your Feet

    Elevators can be associated with boredom and waiting. We attempt to alleviate some of this expected boredom by changing up the way one interacts with an elevator and bringing attention to the related somatic experiences that normally go unnoticed

    September 2020

    Dark Patterns in Web Design

    Discussing the ethics of design practices using the example of “dark patterns” in contemporary web design: features and choices which aim to deceive the user in order to achieve a goal related to corporate or political profit.

    January 2021

    Furhat Culinary Instructor

    This report outlines the details of a project and accompanying pilot study that focused on the effect of using a Furhat Culinary Instructor helper robot when learning a new recipe.

    January 2020

    Precipitation Data Processing and Visualisation

    This document presents the results of our thesis project: an automatic web system for downloading, processing, and visualizing precipitation data obtained via NASA’s GPM Mission.

    October 2020

    Cloud Connected

    A workbook presenting the design concept for a productivity system centered around a smart desktop lamp called "The Cloud." The system utilizes various interactions to help create a more efficient, less stressful home workspace.

    November 2021

    Essay: Climate Lollygagging

    A short essay for a seminar assignment discussing sustainability and climate issues.

    September 2021

    Standing On Your Feet Design Diary

    A design diary about the process of creating the elevator concept in the Standing On Your Feet project. Contains various artifacts of that process (such as art and body maps) and reflections on the steps to the final result.

    June 2019

    Style Transfer from Artworks to Photographs

    Presenting a program for style transfer from one image to another, using two images as input ("style" and "content") and outputting a filtered version of the content image, changed to emulate the chosen style.

    May 2019

    Transphobic Speech Classifier

    In this project, our goal was to observe the prevalence of transphobic content online, while also applying natural language processing methods to classify a specific “subgenre” of hate speech in practice.

    December 2020

    Science Education vs Learning Science

    A short essay discussing the flaws and pitfalls of contemporary education in the sciences and how the experience of students could improve.

    Script for the Yunak game demo
